Questions To Ask Customers

  1. How many TVs do you need mounted?
  2. For each TV: what size is it (under 65" or 65"+)?
  3. If the TV is 65" or larger: can someone at the home help lift it onto the mount?
  4. Do you already have a mount, or do you need one? (fixed = flat against wall, articulating = tilts/swivels)
  5. What kind of wall is it going on — drywall or brick?
  6. Do you want the wires/cables hidden in the wall? (requires an existing outlet near/below the TV location)
  7. What city are you in?
  8. What day/time works best for you?
